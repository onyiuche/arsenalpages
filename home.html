Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Holloway, London, who play in the Premier League, the highest level of English football. One of the most successful clubs in English football, they have won 13 First Division and Premier League titles and a joint record 11 FA Cups.

Arsenal have one of the highest incomes and largest fanbases in the world. The club was named the fifth most valuable association football club in the world, valued at 1.3 billion Euros in 2014

Arsenal fans are know as Gunners or Gooners, and are the best you would ever come across. They show true character in and out of the stadium, as well as in person
